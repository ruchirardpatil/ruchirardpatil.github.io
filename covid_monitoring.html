• Project Title:
    Covid Patient Monitoring
  • Tech Stack:
    Internet of Things(IoT), Tinkercad and ThingSpeak

Project Details

  • Leveraged the ThingSpeak platform for efficient record maintenance, integrating it with servo motor technology to automate door operations. This not only optimized system functionality but also improved overall operational efficiency, enabling seamless control of the environment.

  • Integrated a buzzer system to provide alarm notifications for critical events, while also developing a medicine reminder system utilizing a segment display. Additionally, implemented sensor technology to regularly monitor temperature and oxygen levels, ensuring timely health alerts and contributing to continuous patient monitoring.
